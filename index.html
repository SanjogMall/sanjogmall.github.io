Who I am

I am a proficient data engineer with hands-on experience in cloud technologies such as AWS and GCP, diverse databases including MySQL, PostgreSQL, Redshift, and BigQuery, and containerization tools like Docker and Kubernetes. Additionally, I am well-versed in workflow orchestration with Airflow, CI/CD practices, and various big data frameworks including Hadoop and Spark.

Beyond my technical expertise, I am an enthusiastic explorer constantly seeking new experiences and knowledge to expand my horizons. My portfolio reflects both my passion for discovery and my ability to leverage these skills to optimize data workflows, highlighting the endless possibilities that arise from pushing boundaries and embracing the unknown.

What I do

In my role as a data engineer, I design and develop robust data pipelines to efficiently extract, transform, and load (ETL) data from various sources into databases and data warehouses. These pipelines are essential for ensuring the integrity and accessibility of the organization's data. Additionally, I specialize in data migration projects, facilitating the seamless transfer of data between different systems while minimizing downtime and ensuring data consistency.

I also focus on optimizing data storage and retrieval by implementing efficient database structures and data warehousing solutions. This involves designing schemas, indexing strategies, and partitioning schemes to support fast and scalable access to data. Furthermore, I work on real-time streaming platforms to enable the processing of data as it is generated, allowing for immediate insights and actions. As part of my responsibilities, I perform data modeling to define the structure and relationships of the organization's data, ensuring it meets the needs of various stakeholders. Additionally, I conduct data cleansing, validation, and quality checks to identify and correct any inaccuracies or inconsistencies in the data, ensuring its reliability for analysis and decision-making purposes.
